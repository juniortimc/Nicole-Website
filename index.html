More Than Just a Brand

Founded by Nicole Venter, a graduate with a Diploma in Fashion Design from Elizabeth Galloway, unwritten is a celebration of slow fashion and intentional design born from the heart of South Africa.

Every piece begins as a quiet idea — a sketch on paper, a drape of linen, a conversation between maker and material. Our garments are handcrafted by local artisans who pour generations of skill into every stitch, honouring a tradition of quality that refuses to be rushed.

Our mission is empowering individuals to break free from limitations and forge their own path. We believe clothing should carry a story: of the hands that shaped it, the fabrics chosen with care, and the woman who wears it with grace. This is fashion without compromise — thoughtful, timeless, and unmistakably yours.
